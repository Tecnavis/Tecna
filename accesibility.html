accesibility

At tecnavis, we are committed to making our software accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to ensure that our website and applications are usable and inclusive for all users, regardless of their abilities or assistive technologies they may use. This accessibility page provides information about our efforts to achieve accessibility and the features we have implemented to enhance the user experience.

1. Keyboard Navigation:

Our software can be fully operated using a keyboard, ensuring that individuals who cannot use a mouse or other pointing device can navigate through the appl- ication effectively.

2. Screen Reader Compatibility:

We have designed our software to work seamlessly with screen readers, enabling users with visual impairments to access and interact with the content using assistive technology.

3. Contrast and Color Scheme:


We have carefully chosen color combinations and contrast levels to ensure that the text and interface elements are easily readable for users with visual impairments or color vision deficiencies.

4. Alternative Text for Images:


All images within our software include descriptive alternative text, allowing screen readers to convey the information to users who cannot see the images.

5. Clear and Consistent Layout:

We have maintained a clear and consistent layout throughout our software, making it easier for users with cognitive or learning disabilities to navigate and understand the content.

6. Captions and Transcripts:

Any multimedia content, such as videos or audio, are accompanied by captions or transcripts, ensuring that users who are deaf or hard of hearing can access the information

Accessibility Testing and Feedback

We conduct regular accessibility audits and usability testing to identify and address any barriers to accessibility. Our team of accessibility experts works diligently to ensure that our software adheres to the best practices and guidelines. However, we understand that there is always room for improvement, and we encourage users to provide feedback regarding any accessibility issues they encounter. If you have any suggestions, questions, or concerns about the accessibility of our software, please contact us at [email or contact form]. Your feedback is invaluable in helping us improve and ensure a positive experience for all users.

Conclusion
----------

We are committed to making our software accessible to everyone, and we will continue to invest in accessibility improvements.
